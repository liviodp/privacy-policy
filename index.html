Introduction

BirthdayReminder ("the App") is developed and published by Livio Del Puppo ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Our commitment: BirthdayReminder is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device — we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data We Collect

Data You Provide Directly

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
First name & last name Display birthday contacts Local device only
Birth date (day, month) Calculate upcoming birthdays Local device only
Birth year (optional) Calculate and display age Local device only
Photo (optional) Display contact picture Local device only

Data Generated by the App

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Notification preferences Schedule birthday reminders Local device only
App settings Remember your preferences Local device only

Data We Do NOT Collect

BirthdayReminder does not collect, access, or transmit:

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports to external services
  • Any data to third-party servers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial information
  • Browsing history

Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions, only when needed:

Notifications (Optional)

  • Why: To send you birthday reminders on the day of each birthday
  • When asked: First time you add a birthday contact
  • If denied: The App works normally, but you won't receive reminders

Camera (Optional)

  • Why: To take a photo for a birthday contact
  • When asked: When you tap the camera option in the photo picker
  • If denied: You can still select photos from your library

Photo Library (Optional)

  • Why: To select an existing photo for a birthday contact
  • When asked: When you tap to add a photo to a contact
  • If denied: You can still use the App without photos

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is protected by iOS device encryption
  • Is included in your iCloud or iTunes backups (if enabled)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App

No Cloud Sync (V1.0)

The current version does not include cloud synchronization. Your data exists only on the device where you created it.

Data Retention

Your data persists until you:

  • Delete individual birthday contacts within the App
  • Uninstall the App from your device
  • Reset your device

Third-Party Services

BirthdayReminder does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. There are:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No external APIs

Children's Privacy

BirthdayReminder does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App is suitable for all ages as it stores data locally and does not transmit any information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us. Since all data is stored locally on the device, you can directly delete any information by removing birthday contacts or uninstalling the App.

Your Rights

For All Users

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data directly in the App
  • Rectify: Edit any birthday contact at any time
  • Delete: Remove any or all birthday contacts
  • Portability: Your data is accessible via iOS backups

For European Users (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have additional rights including:

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, you exercise these rights directly through the App or by uninstalling it.

For California Users (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes
  • We do not use your personal information for profiling

Data Breach Notification

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers, the risk of a server-side data breach is eliminated. Your data security depends on your device's security settings (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).
